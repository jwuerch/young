Meet the Team

Dr. Young and his team will make you feel more comfortable than you ever thought possible.

Robert M. Young, DDS, PA

Dr. Robert Young graduated in 2004 from Baylor College of Dentistry, with undergrads in Environmental Biology and Premed Biology. He has an active passion for Dentistry and believes in treating his patients with the type of family style care he would want to receive himself. He believes in taking the time to listen to his patients so he can incorporate their wants with their actual dental health needs. He has an avid passion for learning and is constantly striving to stay ahead with continuing education.

He began practicing in Asheboro, NC in 2004 and left there in 2007. Dr. Young was able to open his dream practice, Brassfield Cosmetic & Family Dental Center May 2008 in Greensboro, NC. In the time since opening he has continually been growing his practice and has been able to increase his staff to accommodate all the incoming new patients. From 2007 until July 2016 Dr. Young was involved with Access Dental. This program had him working in nursing homes and special needs facilities throughout the triad providing dental care. After opening Brassfield Dental he continued to work with Access Dental on Fridays because of how much he enjoyed that special outreach opportunity, leaving only because of the continued growth here at Brassfield Dental.

He has been married to his wife Karen for over 25 years. He is a father to daughters Megan and Andrea. Andrea is married to Marcus and they spend a lot of their free time with Dr. Young and his wife Karen. In his free time he enjoys gardening, running, traveling and spending his time with his family.

Kimberly Baker, Office Manager

Kimberly began her career in finance and then transitioned into the dental field in 2004 with Dr. Young in Asheboro, NC. She has worked in office management, insurance and finance since 1997.

She came back to join Dr. Young’s team at Brassfield Dental in March 2015. Her goal is to help him continually grow his practice and help patients achieve their dream smile while assisting with the insurance and financial aspects of obtaining their treatment plans. Her door is always open to patients to answer any questions regarding their treatment plan, insurance or anything they may need to feel comfortable as patients here at Brassfield Dental.

She has two teenage sons, Jacob and Kobe. She enjoys spending her free time with them, friends and family. She loves to travel and her favorite travel place is anywhere near the ocean.

Shannon King, Front Desk / Dental Assistant II

Shannon is a 1999 graduate of Alamance Community College with a certificate in Dental Assisting. She began working in the dental field in 1999 in Burlington until she moved to Greensboro and decided to transition to a scheduling front desk position in the dental field.

She joined Dr. Young’s team in July 2016 and is the first smile patients see when they visit with us.

She has two sons, Austin and Tristan and is a grandmother to Austin’s son Adrian. She is a proud Air Force parent of Austin as he joined the Air Force as a firefighter in 2014. She enjoys spending time with her family and her great Dane Thor.

Cyndi Murphy, Registered Dental Hygienist

Cyndi is a 1992 graduate of Guilford Technical Community College with a certificate in Dental Assisting. She later returned to GTCC to study Dental Hygiene and graduated in 1998. She was the recipient of the Golden Scaler Award. She has primarily focused her career towards educating her patients about the prevention of periodontal disease. Cyndi has had many years’ experience working in the periodontal field and is proficient in all aspects of periodontal treatment as an assistant and a hygienist. She also enjoyed working as an Adjunct Instructor at Montgomery Community College in the Dental Assisting Department.

Cyndi joined Dr. Young’s team in January 2009 and has progressed to create a full time dental hygiene schedule within his practice. She loves building continued relationships with her patients and helping them maintain their healthy smiles.

Cyndi is married to Chad and they have a daughter, Caylin.

Heather Crutchfield, Registered Dental Hygienist

Heather graduated from Guilford Technical Community College as a Registered Dental Hygienist in 2008. She has worked full time since graduating.

She joined Dr. Young’s team as a part time employee in October 2012, becoming full time in October 2016 making our Hygiene team complete. Our patients love her smile and easy going personality as she helps educate them in keeping their smiles healthy.

She is married to husband Robby and they have a daughter, Camryn. Heather loves spending time with her family and traveling as much as time allows.

Ashley Pritchett, Dental Assistant II

Ashley graduated from Guilford Technical Community College in Dental Assisting in 2000. She began working with Dr. Young in Asheboro, NC in 2004 and has been with him since that time moving from Asheboro, NC to help open Brassfield Cosmetic & Family Dental Center.

From 2007 until July 2016 Ashley was involved with Access Dental with Dr. Young. This program had her working in nursing homes and special needs facilities throughout the triad providing dental care. She continued working with Access Dental on Fridays after Brassfield Dental opened because of how much she enjoyed that special outreach opportunity, leaving only because on the continued growth here at Brassfield Dental.

She has been married to husband Keith for over 15 years. Ashley loves meeting new patients. She loves to see how dentistry can change a person’s life and perception of themselves.

Lorrie Spurlin, Certified Dental Assistant

Lorrie started her work in the dental field in 1985 working in a Dental crown and bridge lab. In 1989 she went on to become a Certified Dental Assistant and has worked full time since.

She has worked with Dr. Young since 2004 starting with him in Asheboro, NC and then moving with him to help open Brassfield Cosmetic & Family Dental Center. Lorrie loves the opportunity that dentistry gives her to help change people’s lives.

She is married to husband Greg and together they have 3 daughters, Maleah, Kayly, and Taylor. She is a MawMaw to Maleah’s daughter Kynsleigh and enjoys spending her free time with her and family as much as possible.
